Who Are We?

The Pigeon Coop is an assortment of cabins located along the shores of beautiful lake Tobin, Canada. We specialize in catering to the hunting and fishing demographic of tourists visiting Canada to enjoy the great outdoors. Visitors to the Pigeon Coop cabins will find a variety of cabins that can be used as a base of operations for groups of hunters or fishermen, or even as a relaxing getaway for the outdoors-loving family. Our cabins vary in size and price, with a total available selection of 6 cabins, each housing between 4 and 12 people. In addition to the fair weather crowd, we also remain open throughout the winter months to allow for the ultimate ice-fishing getaway. For convenience, all payments are conducted online, and refunds are not allowed when cancelling a reservation less than 3-months away.

When you visit a Pigeon Coop cabin, you are experiencing the best the great Canadian outdoors has to offer. Hunting, fishing, kayaking, skiing, hiking, biking, rafting, and so much more are all available and within reasonable distances. In addition, we run a local diner/game-hall thats situated within 5 miles of all our cabins. This main lodge is open to all, serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The main lodge (pictured below) also has an assortment of pool tables, board games, and a dedicated bar. When bringing a boat, our customers can use the landing at the main lodge, or you can rent paddle-boats, canoes, kayaks, and various water-toys. We think you'll love the cozy feel of our cabins and the great adventures possible starting right at the doorstep, we encourage you to check out the different cabins available for rental, as well as the activities and attractions listed on the attractions page.

Who Am I?

My name is Jake Dawson. I am a senior at the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire persuing a major in Software Engineering and a minor in Physics. In academics, I've always been most interested in STEM fields like robotics and natural sciences. Throughout my years in college, I've taken an interest in fields ranging from World History, to Circuits , to Software Development. After my first C++ class, I knew that the software field was the field for me. After starting the program, I've had experience developing software in languages such as Java, C, C++, Ruby, Scheme, and even some low-level MIPs!

The area I most enjoyed was web-development; learning HTML, Javascript, and some PHP to bring designs to life right before your eyes is one of the most satisfying areas I've come across in the whole of my journey so far. In addition to the education recieved at my University, I've also dabbled with developing simple iOS apps and spent time on learning some different web technologies like Bootstrap, as well as some different web-development tricks like lightboxes. Visual design for user interfaces is easily my favorite area of computer science so far, its a super interesting mix of art and technology that is fun to develop and use!

Beyond academics and software in general, my biggest passions are reading in the fantasy genre, all things involving the Green Bay Packers, and obsessing over anything to do with outer space. If you give me the chance, I will aboslutely try and spread the gospel that is some of my favorite author's works! Some of my favorite players on the Packers include greats like Reggie White (aka "The Minister of Defense!"), Donald Driver, Brett Favre, Jordy Nelson, Charles Woodson, and of course Aaron Rodgers. Hopefully, you've gleaned a good image of me from these descriptions, please feel free to contact me via the above "Contact Me" button or directly through the means provided at the bottom of the page!
